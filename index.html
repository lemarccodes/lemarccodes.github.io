About Me

Lemarc Deneal September, born in Riversdale, Western Cape, South Africa is a full-time BCom student at Stellenbosch University. From a government school to an A.C.E school, September has navigated his way through very different schooling systems and managed to matriculate with five distinctions.

Apart from academics, September has always had a keen interest in music and started developing his own music from the age of seven. At the age of 17, September debuted one of his original songs on Expresso, a South African morning show on national television.
